# Reading Time Plus

Add an accurate "X min read" estimate to your posts automatically, or anywhere
with a shortcode. Part of the
[Zub Plugin Factory](https://github.com/ZubeidHendricks/wp-plugin-factory).

## What it does

Calculates reading time from your chosen words-per-minute and displays it before
or after post content, or via the `[reading_time]` shortcode. Configurable
label (`{time} min read`), scope (posts or posts+pages), and optional per-image
time (Pro).

## Settings

**Settings → Reading Time Plus** — words per minute, label, position, scope.
