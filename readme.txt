Add an accurate "X min read" estimate to your posts automatically, or anywhere with a shortcode.

== Description ==

Reading Time Plus shows readers how long an article will take, which lifts
engagement and reduces bounce. It calculates from your chosen reading speed and
displays automatically before or after the content — or wherever you drop the
`[reading_time]` shortcode.

**Free features**

* Automatic "X min read" before or after content.
* Configurable words-per-minute and label (use {time}).
* Posts only, or posts and pages.
* `[reading_time]` shortcode for themes and page builders.

**Pro features**

* Add per-image reading time.
* Priority support.

== Installation ==

1. Install via Plugins → Add New, or upload the zip.
2. Activate.
3. Configure under Settings → Reading Time Plus.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How is the time calculated? =

Word count divided by your reading-speed setting (default 200 wpm), rounded up,
minimum one minute.

= Can I place it manually? =

Yes — set Auto-display to "Don't auto-display" and use the `[reading_time]`
shortcode wherever you want it.
